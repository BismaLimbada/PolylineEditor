Project Scope & Goals

The objective is to move away from static "pixel-based" drawing toward a "vector-based" approach. By treating lines as mathematical polylines, we enable non-destructive editing.

  • High precision coordinate tracking.
  • Real-time manipulation of geometry.
  • Scalable data storage (up to 100 polylines).

Ethnography & User Insight

Observation of students performing geography coordinate plotting reveals that 80% of errors occur during the "final" ink phase. Our requirements prioritize Editability to reduce user frustration.

Core Functional Requirements

Interaction Triggers

  • 'b' (Begin): Starts a new polyline sequence.
  • 'm' (Move): Allows repositioning of the closest vertex.
  • 'd' (Delete): Removes the closest vertex and heals the line.

System Constraints

  • Screen Redraw ('r') for state cleanup.
  • Euclidean Distance calculation for selection.
  • Safe Exit ('q') to terminate session.
